GALLO

El que le cantó a san Pedro no le volverá a cantar.

In Lotería, El Gallo (The Rooster) is a vibrant and lively card, symbolizing courage, pride, and the beginning of a new day. The rooster's crow is associated with the dawn, making it a symbol of hope and energy. It’s one of the most iconic cards in the game, reflecting the importance of the rooster in Mexican culture.
