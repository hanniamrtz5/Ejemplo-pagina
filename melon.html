MELON

"El melón y sus olores, un pedazo me has de dar."

"The Melón card in Lotería represents sweetness and refreshment. Depicted as a ripe melon, this card is often associated with summer and the joy of enjoying fresh, seasonal fruits. In Mexican culture, the melon is a symbol of abundance and natural sweetness, evoking images of family gatherings and the simple pleasures of life."
