Bandera

"Bonito cinco de mayo, el pabellón nacional."

"The Bandera card in Lotería represents national pride and unity. Depicted as the Mexican flag, it symbolizes the country's history, culture, and the spirit of its people. The flag is a powerful emblem of Mexico’s identity, often displayed during celebrations, national holidays, and moments of collective pride. It serves as a reminder of the values of independence, freedom, and solidarity."
